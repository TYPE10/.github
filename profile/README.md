TYPE10 Media
============

Software & cloud consultancy · Munich

> Senior engineering for platforms that scale

## ℹ️ About Us
TYPE10 Media is a senior engineering consultancy. For over 15 years we've architected,
built and scaled streaming platforms, marketplaces and comparison engines — for clients
like maxdome, AutoScout24 and CHECK24 — and we ship our own products, too.

## 🚀 Services
- **Cloud & DevOps**: Cloud architecture, Kubernetes, CI/CD and observability that keep production calm.
- **Backend & Microservices**: Distributed service backends and data layers built for throughput and reliability.
- **Web Frontends**: Fast, accessible React frontends and design systems your team can own.
- **Blockchain & Web3**: Smart contracts, dApps and integrations — pragmatic, audited, production-grade.
- **Product Engineering**: From MVP to scale: we build and run SaaS products, including our own.
- **Applied AI**: LLM assistants and autonomous agents that do real work inside your product — not bolted-on chatbots.

## 🔁 Workflow
We employ an agile workflow, ensuring flexibility, transparency, and collaboration from
conception to completion. Our iterative approach guarantees continuous improvement and
refinement, aligning the final product precisely with client expectations and emerging
market trends.

We design and operate Vitao: an ATS-optimized résumé builder with AI assistance and a fair
one-time price. It's how we prove our product-engineering practice on our own dime.
